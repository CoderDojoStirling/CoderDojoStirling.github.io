We are CoderDojo Stirling

CoderDojo Stirling is a free monthly coding club for kids in Stirling, Scotland.

If you're aged between 7 and 17 and want to learn how to code, then come along.

If you're a grown-up instead, then come help out!

Come Along Monthly

We run one Saturday a month at Stirling CodeBase. You'll need a ticket.
