Helping Out

Are you a grown-up with coding skills? Then become a CoderDojo mentor!

CoderDojo Stirling is run by volunteers. Every month we put on a coding club for kids, with grown-up mentors helping out.

If you're interested in helping out as a mentor, then email us.

You don't need to have wizard-like programming skills - just enough to help the kids as they learn.

Take a look at our Earn Badges page to see what the kids do at CoderDojo. Being a mentor is fun, and you learn a surprising amount!

Go on, email us. You know you want to.
