Helping Out

Are you a grown-up with coding skills? Then become a CoderDojo mentor!

CoderDojo Stirling is run by volunteers. Every month we put on a coding club for kids, with grown-up mentors helping out.

You don't need to have wizard-like programming skills, just enough to help the kids out as they learn. You can take a look at our "Earn Badges" page to see what kind of things the kids do at CoderDojo. Being a mentor is fun, and you learn a surprising amount!

We're always welcome to new members and ideas for what new programming challenges we can set the kids.

If you're interested in helping out as a mentor, then email us.
