Attending CoderDojo Stirling

We run one Saturday a month at Stirling Old Town Jail.

You will need a ticket, as places are limited.

We run two separate Saturday sessions for 14 kids each: the first 12pm - 2pm, the second 3pm - 5pm.

Tickets for upcoming dojos are available through Eventbrite.
