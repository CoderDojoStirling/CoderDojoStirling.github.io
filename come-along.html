Come Along

Everyone is welcome!!

Sept 2022 update: after a Covid-related pause, we restarted CoderDojo Stirling in Nov 2021. We are now running on the fixed schedule below, with reduced numbers per session (currently 14 kids). Email us if you want to be notified of upcoming dojos. We'll add you to our mailing list.


Attending CoderDojo Stirling

We run the last Saturday each month at Stirling Codebase.

There are 2 sessions, each for 14 kids. The morning session is 9:30am - 11:30am, the afternoon session is 12pm - 2pm.

You'll need a ticket, as places are limited.


How to book tickets

Tickets for upcoming dojos are available through TicketTailor.

Please email us if you want to be notified of upcoming dojos. We'll add you to our mailing list.


What to bring

Bring:

  • Yourself
  • A laptop (if you have one)
  • Headphones (if you have some)
We have some spare laptops and headphones to borrow - but not enough for everyone.


Attending other Coderdojos

If you can't attend the sessions at Stirling CoderDojo, then there other dojos running in Scotland and elsewhere.

The CoderDojo Scotland website has details of Scotland dojos, or try the CodeDojo global website for dojos further afield.
