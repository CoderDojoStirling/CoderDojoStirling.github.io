Attending CoderDojo Stirling

We run the last Saturday a month at Stirling Old Town Jail.

There are two separate sessions for 14 kids each: the first 12pm - 2pm, the second 3pm - 5pm.

You'll need a ticket, as places are limited.

How to book tickets

Tickets for upcoming dojos are available through Eventbrite.

Please email us if you want to be notified of upcoming dojos. We'll add you to our mailing list.

What to bring

Bring:

  • Yourself
  • A laptop (if you have one)
  • Headphones (if you have some)

We have some spare laptops and headphones to borrow - but not enough for everyone.

Attending other Coderdojos

If you can't attend the sessions at Stirling CoderDojo, then there other dojos running in Scotland and elsewhere.

The CoderDojo Scotland website has details of Scotland dojos, or try the CodeDojo global website for dojos further afield.
